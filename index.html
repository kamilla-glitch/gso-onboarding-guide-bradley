W1

Welcome to GSO & New to Square

Setup, systems access, team orientation & tools

Please spend your first week at Square getting set up with your new laptop, gaining access to all required systems, and familiarising yourself with Square and Block as a business. At the same time, please spend time familiarising yourself with the GSO team mission, structure, goals, and resources. Get to know the basics of being a new employee at Square, as a GSO rep, and get to know your team and your Sidekick, Kristian Bojsen-Moller.

Your Sidekick is your go-to person for questions, guidance, and support as you find your feet. Kristian is there to help you navigate the team, the tools, and the day-to-day — don't hesitate to lean on him early and often.

Throughout your onboarding guide we will refer you to the GSO Playbook (go/gsoplaybook) — this is your source of truth for anything you need to know as a new Onboarding Specialist. Please bookmark this for future reference. It will be updated continuously.

GSO Tooling
ℹ️ This section covers the fundamental tools, platforms, and systems every new team member must set up in Week 1. These are critical for communication, scheduling, documentation, and daily operations.
🌐

Chrome Extensions

  • ChiliPiper — Booking tool for all seller-facing meetings. Install the Chrome extension for quick access to your booking links.
  • Text Blaze — Snippet tool for templating common responses, emails, and Salesforce notes. Saves significant time on repetitive comms.
  • Highspot — Sales enablement platform. The extension lets you share approved content directly from your browser.
  • go/links — Internal URL shortener. Install the extension so go/ links resolve automatically in Chrome without needing to be on VPN.
🔑

Registry Roles

go/registry ↗

Registry manages system access and role-based permissions across Square. You'll need to request the correct roles to access the tools and data required for your role.

Request the following roles in Registry:

  • Professional Services Team — Grants access to GSO-specific Salesforce views, dashboards, and queue management tools.
  • Team Business - Australia — Provides access to AU market data, reporting, and business-level account information.
🌶️

ChiliPiper

ChiliPiper is the booking tool for all seller-facing meetings. Every kickoff call, follow-up, and seller appointment must be booked through ChiliPiper to ensure proper tracking and attribution.

  • Book 30-minute kickoff calls with a 30-minute buffer after each session to complete notes and Salesforce updates.
  • Book follow-up appointments during the kickoff call using ChiliPiper — don't let sellers leave without a next step booked.
  • Sync your ChiliPiper booking link with your Google Calendar to keep availability accurate at all times.
⚙️ ChiliPiper Account Setup 🎓 ChiliPiper Training (Live) 📖 ChiliPiper Training (Self-Guided)
⚠️ For ChiliPiper setup support, work with Macarena Castillo. Required to be in use by 7 April 2025.

Salesforce

go/salesforce ↗

Salesforce is the CRM used by the Revenue team to manage seller accounts, opportunities, and deal support requests. All GSO activity is logged and tracked here.

Account hierarchy:

  • Parent Account → Account → Opportunity → Deal Support Request

If you don't have access:

  • Submit an IT support ticket requesting Salesforce access for your role.
  • Allow up to 24 hours for access to be provisioned and processed.
  • Verify your access at go/salesforce once you receive confirmation.
  • If access hasn't arrived after 24 hours, follow up directly with IT via #itsupport on Slack.
✅ Before taking escalations, you'll need to create filters to manage your queue. Let Kamilla Schou Warr know when you're ready.
📅

Calendars & PTO Management

All leave must be submitted via Workday. Follow the notice guidelines below to ensure cover can be arranged and the team is informed.

  • Submit PTO at least 1 week in advance for absences of 3 days or fewer.
  • Submit PTO at least 2 weeks in advance for absences of 4 or more days.
  • Block your Google Calendar as "Out of Office" for the duration of your leave so your availability is visible to the team.
  • Two days before your PTO starts, coordinate handover with Kamilla using the PTO template at go/gsopto.
📋 go/gsopto (PTO Template) 📅 AU GSO PTO Calendar
🛡️

Regulator

go/regulator ↗

Regulator is Square's internal platform for account access and administration. It allows the GSO team to view and manage seller account details, permissions, and configurations that are not visible in Salesforce.

Visit go/regulatoraccess for step-by-step guidance on requesting the correct access level for your role.
